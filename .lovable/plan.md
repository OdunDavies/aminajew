
# Luxury Jewelry Showcase Website

## Design
- **Style**: Dark tones, gold accents, serif fonts — high-end luxury aesthetic
- **Color palette**: Deep black/charcoal backgrounds, gold (#D4AF37) accents, white text
- **Typography**: Serif headings (elegant feel), clean sans-serif body text

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- Full-screen hero with stunning jewelry imagery and tagline
- Featured collections carousel
- "New Arrivals" and "Best Sellers" sections
- Brand story snippet
- Newsletter signup

### 2. Collections Pages
- **4 categories**: Rings, Necklaces, Bracelets, Earrings
- Grid layout with hover effects showing quick details
- Filter by price range, material, and style
- Sort options (price, newest, popularity)

### 3. Product Detail Page
- Large image gallery with zoom
- Product name, description, price, material details
- Size/variant selector
- "Add to Cart" button
- Related products section

### 4. Shopping Cart
- Slide-out cart drawer
- Quantity adjustments, remove items
- Order summary with subtotal
- Proceed to checkout button

### 5. Checkout & Payments
- Delivery address form
- Shipping options selection
- **Stripe integration** for secure card payments
- Order confirmation page

### 6. Delivery Features
- Shipping cost calculation based on location
- Order tracking status page
- Delivery estimates displayed on product pages

### 7. Additional Pages
- **About Us** — brand story and craftsmanship
- **Contact** — form and details
- **FAQ** — shipping, returns, care instructions

## Backend (Supabase + Stripe)
- Product catalog with images, prices, variants
- Shopping cart and order management
- User accounts (optional)
- Stripe checkout for payments
- Order tracking and delivery status

## Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with logo, collection links, search, cart icon
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Footer with links, social media, newsletter
